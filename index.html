How To 6Mans

Commands For Queues
  • /q - Adds you to the queue
  • /l - Removes you from the queue
  • /q - Shows who is currently in the queue

You cannot be in a tiered queue and the casual queue at the same time

Commands For Win Reporting
  • /win[GAME ID] - reports which team won

Only 1 player has to do this. Use the game name that the bot dms you. E.g /win RLI123

Commands For Leaderboard Spam
  • /leaderboard - shows the leaderboard of the tier you're in
  • /stats - shows your 6mans stats for the player you enter

The Bot
  • Waits till there's 6 people in the queue
  • Tags the people in the queue, when there are 6 in the queue
  • A vote will come up to choose between a random game, a captains game, and a balanced game
  • If it's a captains game, the bot will not autofill the teams
  • Dms all 6 players the lobby details
  • When game is finished only 1 player from the winning team reports the win into win-reporting
  • leaderboard-spam is there for people to show off afterwards

General Notes
  • Make sure to leave the queue if you can no longer play, staying in the queue and going afk ruins it for everyone
  • Games should be bo5 to encourage people to play with as many different people / combinations of teams as possible
  • You cannot change your mind on the vote between captains and random teams so do not misclick

Getting a Tier
  • If you can't see elite-queue, premier-queue, or championship-queue you can gain access by doing a rank check in ranking and tagging @6mans Staff
  • You will then be assigned a tier based on your mmr, please refer to the rulebook for further information
  • If you are a Non-Irish Player who has just joined or who hasn't been slightly active, you must be vouched by an Irish or Integrated Non-Irish member of the server to gain access to 6mans. There is no vouching limit, just be reasonable with this

General Rules
  • Standard 3v3
  • Best of 5
  • Players must be in their team voice call when playing Championship/Premier with a working mic
  • If someone should disconnect during a game the game can only be restarted if:
    1. No goals have been scored
    2. Less than 1 minute has passed
  • If someone fails to show up within 10 minutes for a series, when selecting teams/once teams are picked, the series should not be reported and @6mans Staff should be notified
  • Do not ping the 6mans role within 15 minutes of the last time it was pinged. You can add / remove the 6mans role in set-roles
  • Failure to follow these rules will result in a ban from 6mans time depending on the offence/number of offences
